<p><p>The Peltier modules market is primarily divided into single-stage and multistage modules. Single-stage modules are typically used for compact applications requiring moderate cooling capacities, ideal for electronic cooling and small refrigeration systems. In contrast, multistage modules are designed for higher temperature differentials, making them suitable for specialized applications like laser cooling and thermoelectric temperature stabilization. These distinctions lead to varying market demands, with single-stage modules dominating general applications while multistage modules cater to niche markets with specific performance needs.</p></p>
