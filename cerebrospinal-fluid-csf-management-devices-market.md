<p><p>Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) management devices are crucial for treating conditions related to abnormal CSF accumulation. The market primarily includes CSF shunts, which are implanted devices that redirect excess CSF from the brain to another body cavity, preventing hydrocephalus complications. CSF drainage systems, on the other hand, facilitate the temporary removal of CSF to relieve pressure and manage cerebrospinal fluid disorders. Both device types are pivotal in ensuring effective patient care and improving quality of life for affected individuals.</p></p>
