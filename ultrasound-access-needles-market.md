<p><p>The ultrasound access needles market is primarily categorized into Endoscopic Ultrasound-Fine Needle Aspiration (EUS-FNA) and Endoscopic Ultrasound-Fine Needle Biopsy (EUS-FNB). EUS-FNA is utilized for obtaining cytological samples from cysts or masses, aiding in diagnosis. In contrast, EUS-FNB facilitates the acquisition of larger tissue samples, allowing for more detailed histopathological evaluation. Both needle types enhance minimally invasive procedures, improving diagnostic accuracy and patient outcomes in gastrointestinal and pancreatic disorders. Their demand is growing in clinical practice.</p></p>
