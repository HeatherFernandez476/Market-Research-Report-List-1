<p><strong>The Optical Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Encoders</li><li>Absolute Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical encoder market is primarily segmented into incremental and absolute encoders. Incremental encoders provide relative position changes, generating pulses as the shaft rotates, making them ideal for measuring speed and direction. In contrast, absolute encoders deliver a unique position value for each shaft angle, ensuring precise and accurate readings even after power loss. These types cater to various applications across automation, robotics, and industrial machinery, driven by the demand for precise motion control and improved system efficiency.</p></p>
