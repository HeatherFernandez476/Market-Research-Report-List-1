<p><p>The semiconductor heat treatment furnaces market primarily consists of two types: horizontal and vertical furnaces. Horizontal furnaces are typically used for batch processing, allowing for efficient thermal cycles and uniform temperature distributions across wafers. In contrast, vertical furnaces facilitate high throughput and are ideal for processing multiple layers simultaneously, making them suitable for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. Both types are crucial for ensuring optimal material properties and performance during the production of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
