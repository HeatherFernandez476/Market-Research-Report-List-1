<p><ul><li>Global Constellations</li><li>Regional Constellations</li><li>Satellite-Based Augmentations</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) market is divided into three key types: Global Constellations, Regional Constellations, and Satellite-Based Augmentations. Global Constellations encompass worldwide systems like GPS and GLONASS, providing global coverage. Regional Constellations, such as Galileo and QZSS, focus on specific regions, enhancing local navigation services. Satellite-Based Augmentations (SBAS) improve accuracy and reliability by using ground stations to correct GNSS signals, benefiting applications in aviation, transportation, and agriculture through enhanced positioning capabilities.</p></p>
