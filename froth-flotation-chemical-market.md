<p><p>The froth flotation chemical market comprises three primary types: collectors, frothers, and modifiers. Collectors enhance the hydrophobicity of minerals, promoting their separation from gangue. Frothers stabilize the froth formed during flotation, allowing for better mineral recovery and concentration. Modifiers, including pH regulators and dispersants, adjust the chemical environment to optimize the flotation process for specific minerals. Together, these chemicals improve the efficiency and selectivity of the froth flotation technique, widely used in mineral processing and metal recovery.</p></p>
