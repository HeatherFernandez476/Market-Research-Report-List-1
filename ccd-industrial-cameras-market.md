<p><strong>The CCD Industrial Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CCD industrial cameras market is primarily divided into two types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture a complete image in a single exposure, making them ideal for applications requiring high-resolution imaging of static scenes, such as quality control in manufacturing. In contrast, line scan cameras capture one line of pixels at a time and are suited for high-speed moving objects, commonly used in conveyor systems and continuous monitoring applications. Both types serve diverse industrial needs efficiently.</p></p>
