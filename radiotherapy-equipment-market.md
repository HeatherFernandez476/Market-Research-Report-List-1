<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Radiotherapy Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment</li><li>Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment</li><li>Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiotherapy equipment market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment** employs high-energy beams to target tumors from outside the body, commonly using linear accelerators and cyclotrons.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment**, also known as brachytherapy, involves placing radioactive sources directly inside or near the tumor for localized treatment.</p><p>3. **Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment** delivers radioactive substances through the bloodstream, allowing for targeted treatment of cancers that have spread throughout the body. Each type serves distinct therapeutic purposes in cancer care.</p></p>
