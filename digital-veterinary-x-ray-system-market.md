<p><ul><li>Computed Radiography</li><li>Direct Radiography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Veterinary X-ray System market comprises two main types: Computed Radiography (CR) and Direct Radiography (DR). Computed Radiography utilizes imaging plates to capture X-ray images, which are then digitized for viewing, providing a flexible and cost-effective solution. In contrast, Direct Radiography captures images directly using digital sensors, offering immediate results and enhanced image quality. Both systems enhance diagnostic capabilities in veterinary practices, supporting quicker treatment decisions and improved animal care.</p></p>
