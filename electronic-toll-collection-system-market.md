<p><strong>The Electronic Toll Collection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rfid-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Dsrc-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Video Analytic-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Gnss/Gps-Based ETC Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) System market encompasses various technologies for automated toll collection. RFID-based ETC systems utilize radio frequency identification to identify vehicles, streamlining toll transactions. DSRC-based systems employ Dedicated Short-Range Communication for real-time data exchange between vehicles and toll booths. Video analytic-based systems leverage cameras and image processing to recognize license plates for toll charges. GNSS/GPS-based systems utilize satellite positioning to track vehicle locations, allowing for dynamic toll assessments based on distance traveled.</p></p>
