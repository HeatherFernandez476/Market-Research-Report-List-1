<p><p>Non-invasive ventilation (NIV) masks and circuits are essential components for delivering respiratory support without the need for invasive procedures. NIV masks, including oro-nasal and full-face models, provide a secure fit to ensure effective ventilation. Circuits, on the other hand, consist of flexible tubing and connectors that facilitate gas flow between the ventilator and the mask. Together, they assist patients with respiratory distress by improving airflow and oxygenation while minimizing discomfort and the risk of infections associated with invasive methods.</p></p>
