<p><p>The fire-resistant glass market consists of several key types. Laminated fire-resistant glass features layers of glass bonded with a transparent interlayer, enhancing safety and insulation. Tempered fire-resistant glass undergoes thermal processing for heightened strength and resistance to breakage. Wired fire-resistant glass incorporates a mesh of wire for added stability and fire resistance. Lastly, ceramic fire-resistant glass is made from ceramic materials that can withstand high temperatures without shattering, making it suitable for various safety applications in buildings.</p></p>
