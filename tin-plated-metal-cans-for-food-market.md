<p><p>Tin-plated metal cans for food are primarily categorized into two types: three-piece and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans consist of a body and two ends, typically made from rolled steel, which allows for easier manufacturing and higher customization. Two-piece cans are produced from a single metal sheet, forming the body and bottom in one process, offering a sleeker design and lighter weight. Both types provide excellent barrier properties, ensuring food preservation and safety while promoting ease of transportation and storage.</p></p>
