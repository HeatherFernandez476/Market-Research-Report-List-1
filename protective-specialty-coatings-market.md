<p><strong>The Protective & Specialty Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne Coatings</li><li>Water-borne Coatings</li><li>Powder Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The protective and specialty coatings market comprises various types, including solvent-borne, water-borne, and powder coatings. Solvent-borne coatings utilize organic solvents to dissolve resins, offering durability and high-performance finishes, suitable for various industrial applications. Water-borne coatings, on the other hand, use water as a primary solvent, providing environmentally-friendly options with reduced volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Powder coatings involve applying a dry powder that is cured under heat, creating a tough, uniform finish, often used in automotive and appliance industries.</p></p>
