<p><p>The dental adhesives and sealants market comprises three primary types: water-based, solvent-based, and radiation-cured. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly and easy to use, suitable for various dental applications. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding but may involve volatile organic compounds, affecting health and safety. Radiation-cured adhesives utilize light or heat to trigger curing, providing rapid setting times and robust bonds. Each type caters to different dental procedures, balancing effectiveness, safety, and application convenience based on specific clinical needs.</p></p>
