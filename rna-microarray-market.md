<p><strong>The RNA Microarray Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oligonucleotide RNA Microarrays (oRNA)</li><li>Complementary RNA Microarrays (cRNA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RNA microarray market comprises two main types: oligonucleotide RNA microarrays (oRNA) and complementary RNA microarrays (cRNA). Oligonucleotide microarrays use short, synthetic DNA or RNA probes to detect specific RNA sequences, providing high specificity and sensitivity. Complementary RNA microarrays, on the other hand, utilize labeled cRNA as a hybridization target, allowing for the examination of gene expression levels in a more versatile manner. Both types are crucial for genomic research, diagnostics, and personalized medicine applications.</p></p>
