<p><strong>The Semi-Automatic Vacuum Packaging Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>V-FFS</li><li>H-FFS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-automatic vacuum packaging machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Vertical Form-Fill-Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form-Fill-Seal (H-FFS). V-FFS machines create bags vertically, suitable for products that require quick packaging in a compact space, commonly used for snacks and liquids. In contrast, H-FFS machines operate horizontally, ideal for packaging bulk items and larger products, offering high efficiency and versatility. Both types enhance product shelf life and maintain quality by removing air and sealing packages tightly.</p></p>
