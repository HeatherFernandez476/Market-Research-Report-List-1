<p><p>The feldspathic minerals market includes two primary types: plagioclase feldspar and potassium feldspar. Plagioclase feldspar, characterized by its sodium and calcium content, is essential in the production of glass and ceramics. It's commonly utilized in construction and manufacturing. On the other hand, potassium feldspar, rich in potassium, is mainly used in ceramics and porcelain production, as well as in glass-making, providing strength and durability. Both types serve critical roles across various industrial applications, driving market demand.</p></p>
