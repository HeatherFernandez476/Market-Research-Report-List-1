<p><strong>The Vinyl Extrusion Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Extrusion</li><li>Indirect Extrusion</li><li>Hydrostatic Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vinyl extrusion equipment market can be categorized into three main types: direct extrusion, indirect extrusion, and hydrostatic extrusion. </p><p>Direct extrusion involves forcing heated vinyl through a die to create desired shapes, ensuring efficiency and precision. Indirect extrusion, on the other hand, employs a container to push the material, offering better control over the extrusion process. Hydrostatic extrusion uses high-pressure fluid to shape the vinyl, enabling the production of intricate profiles with minimal internal stress, making it ideal for complex applications.</p></p>
