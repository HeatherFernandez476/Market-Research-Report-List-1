<p><ul><li>Resistive</li><li>Capacitive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sensor signal conditioner (SSC) IC market is categorized primarily into resistive and capacitive types. Resistive signal conditioners are essential for interfacing with resistive sensors, converting resistance changes into voltage or current signals. They are commonly used in temperature sensors and strain gauges. Capacitive signal conditioners, on the other hand, cater to capacitive sensors by processing changes in capacitance to produce readable output signals. These types are vital for applications in pressure sensing, proximity detection, and industrial automation.</p></p>
