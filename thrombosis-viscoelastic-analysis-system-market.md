<p><ul><li>Thromboelastography (TEG)</li><li>Rotational thromboelastometry (ROTEM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thrombosis Viscoelastic Analysis System market includes two primary types: Thromboelastography (TEG) and Rotational Thromboelastometry (ROTEM). TEG analyzes blood coagulation by measuring the viscoelastic properties of whole blood, providing insights into clot formation and stability. ROTEM, on the other hand, employs a rotational approach to assess coagulation status and dynamics in real-time. Both technologies enable rapid, detailed evaluations of hemostasis, enhancing decision-making in clinical settings and improving patient outcomes during surgeries and trauma management.</p></p>
