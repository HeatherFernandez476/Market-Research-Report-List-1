<p><p>The cautery pen market is segmented into high-temperature and low-temperature cautery pens. High-temperature cautery pens are designed to produce intense heat, effectively cutting through tissue and achieving hemostasis in surgical applications, making them ideal for procedures requiring thorough removal or incision. Conversely, low-temperature cautery pens operate at milder temperatures, suitable for delicate procedures where precision and minimized tissue damage are crucial, such as in dermatology or minor surgeries. Each type serves specific medical needs and varies in applications and usage.</p></p>
