<p><ul><li>Activated Carbon Filters</li><li>Ion-exchange Resin Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical special filters market includes various types of filtration technologies, notably activated carbon filters and ion-exchange resin filters. Activated carbon filters effectively remove impurities and contaminants through adsorption, making them ideal for air and water purification. Ion-exchange resin filters, on the other hand, utilize a chemical process to exchange undesirable ions in water with more favorable ones, ensuring the removal of heavy metals and hardness. Both filter types are crucial for maintaining water quality and environmental safety.</p></p>
