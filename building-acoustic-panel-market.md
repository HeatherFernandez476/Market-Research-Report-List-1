<p><p>The building acoustic panel market includes three primary types: Acoustic Membranes, Resonators Panels, and Porous Material Panels. Acoustic Membranes are thin, flexible materials designed to reduce sound transmission. Resonators Panels utilize cavities to absorb specific sound frequencies, enhancing sound quality. Porous Material Panels, made from fibrous or sponge-like materials, dissipate sound energy through absorption, effectively minimizing noise. Each type serves distinct acoustic needs, catering to various applications in commercial and residential settings for improved sound control and comfort.</p></p>
