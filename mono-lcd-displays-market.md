<p><p>Mono LCD displays are categorized into three main types: Character LCDs, Graphic LCDs, and Segmented Glass LCDs. Character LCDs typically feature fixed characters and numerical displays, ideal for simple text and data readouts. Graphic LCDs offer versatility with pixel-based displays, allowing for more complex images and graphics. Segmented Glass LCDs use pre-defined segments for displaying specific characters, often employed in devices like calculators and appliances. Each type serves unique applications across industries, enhancing user interactions with visual information.</p></p>
