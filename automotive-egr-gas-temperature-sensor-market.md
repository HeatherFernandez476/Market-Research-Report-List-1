<p><ul><li>Resistance Type</li><li>Thermocouples Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) gas temperature sensor market encompasses two primary types: resistance type and thermocouple type sensors. Resistance type sensors measure temperature based on changes in electrical resistance of materials, offering high accuracy and stability. Thermocouples, on the other hand, utilize the voltage generated at the junction of two different metals to determine temperature, suitable for high-temperature applications. Both sensor types are crucial for optimizing engine performance, emissions control, and overall vehicle efficiency.</p></p>
