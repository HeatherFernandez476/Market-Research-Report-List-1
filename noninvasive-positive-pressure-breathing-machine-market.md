<p><p>The Noninvasive Positive Pressure Breathing Machine market is segmented into two primary types: fixed and auto. Fixed machines deliver a set pressure level consistently, making them suitable for patients with stable respiratory conditions. In contrast, auto machines automatically adjust pressure levels based on the patient’s breathing patterns, providing personalized treatment for varying respiratory needs. This adaptability can enhance patient comfort and improve outcomes, leading to a growing preference for auto machines in clinical settings and home care.</p></p>
