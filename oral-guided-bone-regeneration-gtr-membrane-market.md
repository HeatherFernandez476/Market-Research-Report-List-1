<p><strong>The Oral Guided Bone Regeneration (GTR) Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable Membrane</li><li>Non-absorbable Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Oral Guided Bone Regeneration (GTR) Membrane market comprises two primary types: absorbable and non-absorbable membranes. Absorbable membranes dissolve over time, eliminating the need for a secondary surgical procedure and promoting natural healing. Non-absorbable membranes, on the other hand, provide long-term support to protect the bone graft but require surgical removal after the healing period. Both types are essential for enhancing bone growth and ensuring successful dental implant placements, catering to varying clinical needs and patient preferences.</p></p>
