<p><p>The 3D IC packaging market consists of three main types: 3D TSV, 2.5D, and 3D Wafer-Level Chip-Scale Packaging (WLCSP). </p><p>3D TSV technology utilizes through-silicon vias to connect multiple stacked die, enhancing performance and reducing footprint. </p><p>2.5D packaging involves placing chips side-by-side on an interposer for improved signal integrity and heat management, facilitating faster communication. </p><p>WLCSP optimizes performance by integrating chips directly onto PCBs, enabling compact designs for portable electronics. Each type addresses specific performance and space constraints in advanced semiconductor applications.</p></p>
