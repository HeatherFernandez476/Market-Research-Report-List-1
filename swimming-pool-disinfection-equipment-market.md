Ultraviolet (UV) disinfection equipment employs UV light to neutralize microorganisms without chemicals. Electrolysis disinfection equipment generates chlorine on-site through electrolysis of saltwater. Other market options may include alternative methods such as mineral sanitizers and ionizers, catering to diverse pool maintenance needs.</p></p>
