<p><ul><li>Assisting Devices</li><li>Implantable Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bariatric surgical devices market comprises two main types: assisting devices and implantable devices. Assisting devices include tools that aid surgeons during bariatric procedures, enhancing precision and safety. Examples are staplers and suturing devices. In contrast, implantable devices are surgically inserted into the patient, designed to promote weight loss by limiting food intake or enhancing metabolic processes. This category includes adjustable gastric bands and intragastric balloons, both of which help patients achieve and maintain significant weight loss.</p></p>
