<p><p>The implantable cardiac devices market encompasses various types aimed at managing heart rhythm disorders. Pacemakers regulate heartbeats by sending electrical impulses, ensuring a steady rhythm. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) detect and treat life-threatening arrhythmias through shocks. Bi-Ventricular ICDs, or Bi-V ICDs, provide advanced therapy by stimulating both heart ventricles, improving heart efficiency in patients with heart failure. Each device addresses specific cardiovascular conditions, contributing to improved patient outcomes and quality of life through innovative technology.</p></p>
