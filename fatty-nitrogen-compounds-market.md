<p><p>The fatty nitrogen compounds market primarily includes three types: primary, secondary, and tertiary fatty amines. Primary fatty amines contain one long-chain fatty group bonded to a nitrogen atom, useful in surfactants and corrosion inhibitors. Secondary fatty amines feature two fatty groups attached to nitrogen, commonly used in agricultural chemicals and textile production. Tertiary fatty amines have three fatty groups, often employed in personal care products and as catalysts in various chemical processes. Each type serves distinct industrial applications, influenced by their molecular structure.</p></p>
