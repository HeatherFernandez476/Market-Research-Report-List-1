<p><p>The dental zirconia block market is categorized into three types based on translucency levels. Low translucency zirconia blocks are designed for posterior restorations, providing high strength but less aesthetic appeal. Medium translucency blocks offer a balance of strength and aesthetics, suitable for anterior and posterior applications. High translucency zirconia blocks are used for highly esthetic restorations, ideal for anterior teeth, providing improved light transmission and a natural appearance. Each type serves specific clinical needs in dental prosthetics.</p></p>
