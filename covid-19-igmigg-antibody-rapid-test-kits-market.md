<p><p>The COVID-19 antibody rapid test kits market comprises three main types: RDT, ELISA, and Neutralization Assay. RDTs offer quick results and are easy to use at the point of care, detecting IgM and IgG antibodies. ELISA tests provide more accurate and quantitative results by measuring antibodies in a laboratory setting, making them suitable for larger scale studies. Neutralization assays determine the ability of antibodies to inhibit virus activity, providing critical insights into immunity and vaccine efficacy. Each plays a vital role in managing the pandemic.</p></p>
