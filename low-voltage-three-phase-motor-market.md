<p><p>The low voltage three-phase motor market primarily includes asynchronous and synchronous motors. Asynchronous motors, commonly known as induction motors, operate based on electromagnetic induction and are widely used due to their robust design and efficiency. Synchronous motors, on the other hand, maintain constant speed regardless of load variations, utilizing direct power supply to their rotor. Both types cater to various industrial applications, providing reliable performance for processes requiring consistent torque and speed control. Their adaptability drives market growth across sectors.</p></p>
