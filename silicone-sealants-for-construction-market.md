<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone sealants for construction are classified mainly into one-component and two-component types. One-component sealants are ready-to-use and cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient for various applications such as glazing, expansion joints, and weatherproofing. In contrast, two-component sealants consist of a base and a hardener that must be mixed before application. This type offers superior strength and flexibility, often used in high-performance settings where resistance to temperature fluctuations and chemicals is essential.</p></p>
