<p><strong>The CRM Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Premise</li><li>Cloud Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CRM software is categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise CRM requires installation on local servers, offering greater control over data and customization, but demands significant IT resources and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based CRM operates on the provider's servers, allowing users to access it via the internet. This model offers scalability, lower upfront costs, and automatic updates, making it ideal for businesses looking for flexibility and ease of use without extensive IT infrastructure.</p></p>
