<p><p>CVD equipment for solar cell production includes various types tailored for specific applications. PECVD (Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition) is used for depositing thin films at lower temperatures, ideal for flexible solar cells. LPCVD (Low-Pressure Chemical Vapor Deposition) offers high-quality films with uniform thickness, suitable for thin-film technologies. MOCVD (Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition) is employed for producing high-efficiency multi-junction cells. Additionally, other CVD methods include HVPE (Hydride Vapor Phase Epitaxy) and ALCVD (Atomic Layer CVD), catering to diverse solar cell manufacturing needs.</p></p>
